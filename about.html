about

the photographer

i've always had an interest in photography since my parents first bought a DSLR camera. since then, i've always brought it along on vacations, usually to take pictures of wildlife (like the two shown here, taken on a vacation to argentina). this project would be my first time actually going out and looking for a vague idea for a photo, capturing whatever inspires me, and then organizing them into a kind of gallery or collection which i think is worth sharing.

the site

a nighttime journey through the streets of the city that never sleeps, from midtown to chinatown and everything in between. discover and learn about the neon signs scattered throughout new yorks many streets and join me as i try to capture the essence of each place's neon-lit charm, one snapshot at a time.
